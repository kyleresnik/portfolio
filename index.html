About Me

Hey there! My name is Kyle Resnik. I'm a JavaScript developer based out of Indianapolis. I'm passionate about art, design, animation, and of course, technology. I'm a very quick learner and I love solving problems with code. I've been tinkering with HTML and CSS since I was about 12 years old, but it wasn't until I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Academy in 2018 that I learned how to channel that hobby into something practical. I graduated from the accelerated JavaScript Program that same year. During the course of this program I learned many skills, but one of the biggest takeaways for me was how many friends I made. I discovered I loved working on projects with a group of other like-minded coders. Currently I am searching for my first role in the industry and am excited to get my foot in the door utilizing the skills I learned.
